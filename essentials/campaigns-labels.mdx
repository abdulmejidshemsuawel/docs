## Campaigns

Campaigns allow you to group related posts together for specific marketing initiatives, product launches, or events.

### Creating Campaigns

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Campaigns">
    Click on the "Campaigns" tab in the main navigation menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create New Campaign">
    Click the "Create Campaign" button to open the campaign creation form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Campaign Details">
    Enter a name, description, and select a color for your campaign. The color will help you quickly identify posts belonging to this campaign in the content calendar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Posts">
    You can add existing posts to your campaign or create new posts directly within the campaign view.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Labels

While campaigns are designed for broader marketing initiatives, labels provide flexible tagging for more granular content categorization.

### Creating Labels

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Labels">
    Click on the "Labels" tab in the side navigation menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create New Label">
    Click the "Create Label" button and enter a name and color for your label.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply to Content">
    Apply labels to individual posts during creation or in bulk to existing posts.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Smart Labeling

PostSyncer offers smart labeling features to help you maintain consistent content categorization:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Auto-Labeling" icon="magic-wand-sparkles">
    Set up rules to automatically apply labels based on post content or media type
  </Card>
  <Card title="Label Suggestions" icon="lightbulb">
    Get AI-powered label suggestions based on your post content
  </Card>
  <Card title="Bulk Labeling" icon="tags">
    Apply the same label to multiple posts at once
  </Card>
  <Card title="Label Templates" icon="rectangle-list">
    Create label sets for different content strategies
  </Card>

### Label Use Cases

Use labels to categorize content by:

| Label Type | Examples | Benefits |
|------------|----------|----------|
| Content Type | #Tips, #BehindTheScenes, #ProductFeature | Track performance by content type |
| Media Format | #Video, #Carousel, #TextOnly | Understand what formats work best |
| Target Audience | #Beginners, #Experts, #Students | Tailor content to specific audience segments |
| Campaign Stage | #Teaser, #Launch, #Promotion | Track content through marketing funnels |
| Platform-Specific | #InstagramOnly, #TwitterThread | Identify platform-exclusive content |
