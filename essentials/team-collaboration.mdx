## Team Roles and Permissions

PostSyncer offers flexible role-based permissions to ensure the right people have the right access:

<img
  src="/images/roles-permissions.png"
  alt="PostSyncer Roles and Permissions"
/>

### Default Roles

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="crown" title="Owner">
    Has full access to all workspace features, including billing and subscription management.
    
    **Permissions:**
    - Manage workspace settings and billing
    - Add/remove team members
    - Create/manage all content
    - Connect/disconnect social accounts
    - Access all analytics and reports
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="user-gear" title="Admin">
    Can manage team members and most workspace settings, but can't change billing.
    
    **Permissions:**
    - Add/remove team members
    - Create/manage all content
    - Connect/disconnect social accounts
    - Access all analytics and reports
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="pen-to-square" title="Creator">
    Can create and edit content, but may require approval before publishing.
    
    **Permissions:**
    - Create and edit posts
    - Schedule content
    - Upload media
    - View analytics
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="eye" title="Reviewer">
    Can view and approve content but cannot create new posts.
    
    **Permissions:**
    - View all scheduled content
    - Approve or reject posts
    - Add comments and suggestions
    - View analytics
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="chart-simple" title="Analyst">
    Has read-only access to analytics and published content.
    
    **Permissions:**
    - View published content
    - Access analytics and reports
    - Download performance data
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Content Approval Workflows

Implement structured approval processes to ensure quality control:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Creator Drafts Post">
    Team member creates content and submits for review.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reviewer Approval">
    Designated reviewers receive notification and can approve, reject, or request changes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Revision Process">
    If changes are requested, the post goes back to the creator with comments.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Final Approval">
    Once approved, the post is scheduled or published according to the set schedule.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Approval Settings

<CardGroup cols={2}>
