## Best Time to Post™

Our proprietary Best Time to Post™ algorithm analyzes your audience's activity patterns to suggest optimal posting times for each platform:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Audience Analysis">
    We analyze when your specific audience is most active on each platform.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Engagement Patterns">
    We identify patterns in engagement (likes, comments, shares) based on post timing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Custom Recommendations">
    We provide personalized time slot recommendations unique to your accounts.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Best Time to Post™ recommendations improve over time as the system gathers more data about your audience's engagement patterns.
</Note>
