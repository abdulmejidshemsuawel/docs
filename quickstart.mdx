## Create Your Account

Getting started with PostSyncer is simple, and you can be up and running in just a few minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up">
    Visit [app.postsyncer.com/register](https://app.postsyncer.com/register) and create your account. You can sign up with your email or use single sign-on with Google.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Your First Workspace">
    A workspace helps you organize your social media accounts. Create a workspace for your brand, agency, or client.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Social Accounts">
    Connect your first social media account by authorizing PostSyncer to publish on your behalf. We use official platform APIs to ensure secure connections.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Connect Your Social Platforms

PostSyncer supports all major social media platforms. Click on the "Connect Account" button in your dashboard to add any of these platforms:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="twitter" title="Twitter / X">
    Connect your Twitter/X account to schedule tweets, threads, and media posts. We support both text-only tweets and posts with images or videos.
    
    **Features:**
    - Schedule tweets with images, videos, or GIFs
    - Create and schedule tweet threads
    - Utilize Twitter cards when sharing links
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="facebook" title="Facebook">
    Connect your Facebook Pages to schedule posts, photos, and videos. Manage multiple pages from one dashboard.
    
    **Features:**
    - Schedule posts to Facebook Pages
    - Upload and share multiple images or videos
    - Customize privacy settings for each post
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="instagram" title="Instagram">
    Connect your Instagram Business account to schedule feed posts, carousels, and stories.
    
    **Features:**
    - Schedule feed posts with images or videos
    - Create multi-image carousel posts
    - Auto-crop images to Instagram's preferred dimensions
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="brands" title="Other Platforms">
    PostSyncer also supports TikTok, YouTube, Pinterest, Threads, Telegram, LinkedIn, and Bluesky. Each platform has specialized features to ensure your content looks perfect on every network.
    
    **Note:** Some platforms may require specific account types (e.g., business accounts) or additional setup steps. Follow the in-app instructions for each platform.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Create Your First Post

Now that your accounts are connected, it's time to create your first post.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Posts">
    Click on the "Posts" section in the main navigation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create New Post">
    Click the "Create Post" button to open the post editor.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Compose Your Content">
    Write your post content, add media (images/videos), and customize settings for each connected platform.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Schedule or Publish">
    Choose to publish immediately or schedule for a future date and time. You can also set up repeating posts for content that needs to be shared multiple times.
  </Step>
</Steps>
